                                                                 STUDENT MARKS AND STUDY PLAN CALCULATOR

Helps students analyze their marks in different subjects.

Provides personalized study recommendations to improve weaker areas.



Steps to use the application

Step 1: Enter subjects and marks.

Step 2: Click Calculate to generate results.

Step 3: View total percentage and subject-wise breakdown.

Step 4: Get improvement strategies and study allocation.


Benefits

Makes students aware of their strengths and weaknesses.
Provides a clear, actionable study plan.
Motivates better time management and focused preparation.
